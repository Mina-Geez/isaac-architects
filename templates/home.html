The Studio

Built on the belief that
architecture is an act
of care

Isaac Architects is an Alexandria practice, founded by Eng. Mina Kamal Isaac. We design for the people who live inside the work.

We hold classical sensibility against contemporary precision — shaped by close attention to context, proportion, and craft. When it's right, it feels both inevitable and surprising.

Our Process

Three principles that
guide every project

01

Listen First

Before a single line is drawn, we listen. To the client's aspirations, the site's whisper, the community's rhythm. Understanding precedes imagination — always.

02

Design Whole

Architecture is not decoration applied to structure. Every element — spatial, structural, material — is resolved as part of one coherent whole, from urban scale to door handle.

03

Build True

The gap between render and reality is where trust lives. We stay through construction, solving problems in real-time, ensuring the built work honors the design promise.
